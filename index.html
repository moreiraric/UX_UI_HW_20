A health app for your dog.

Though we love our furry friends, caring for their health can prove troublesome. BarkBuddy eases that burden by organizing their health information, giving alerts for medications and vaccines, and providing digital storage for every one of their documents.

Research

Research Objectives

  • Find out how people currently manage their dogs' health records and what pain points they have.
  • Understand how people would like to manage their dogs' health records.
  • Which digital tools people use to help them maintain their health.

Our Findings:

  • People want to take great care of their dogs.
  • People would like to have alerts for vaccines.
  • People each have a different way of keeping track of their documents.
  • People often do not have the information they need with them when out and about.

Mapping the experience

It was important to us not to bog down the user with too many steps during the sign up process, so we made it simple. Once the user is signed up and has given some basic information about their dog, they are ready to fill out the information in any order they wish.
